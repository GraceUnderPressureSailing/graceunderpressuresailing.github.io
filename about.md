# About

*Grace Under Pressure* is both a boat and a place to learn how practical marine
systems behave outside the workshop.

This site records projects that have earned a longer life than a private folder
of photographs and notes. The emphasis is on understandable architecture,
evidence from real use, honest limitations and enough context for another owner
to decide whether an idea is relevant to their own vessel.

Source code and suitable supporting material are published through the
[GraceUnderPressureSailing GitHub organisation](https://github.com/GraceUnderPressureSailing).
