# Projects

These are case studies of systems installed or tested aboard *Grace Under
Pressure*. They describe what was done on this particular vessel; they are not
drop-in designs for another boat.

## [LogPrinter](/projects/logprinter/)

A Java 21 and Raspberry Pi appliance for NMEA 2000 capture, live boat state,
thermal printing, replay and passage analysis.

**Status:** In service and recording real passages.

## [Bow-thruster control replacement](/projects/bow-thruster-control/)

Investigation and replacement of an ageing bow-thruster control system, with
particular attention to isolation, interlocks and failure behaviour.

**Status:** Installed; detailed write-up in preparation.
